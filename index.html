Blue Apron Abroad

The World in Your Kitchen

Food that tells a story. Learn about the diverse and rich cultures of the world through food. We provide authentic recipes, ingredients, and utensils to truly eat like a local.

Each month explore a new culture in depth under the guidance of a local cook. You'll receive a new recipe and authentic ingredients each week as well as immersive content and stories to connect with a culture through food.

This Month's Region

Thailand

Explore the culture and cuisine of Thailand with Blue Apron Abroad. Thai practices are heavily influenced by China and India, including food, architecture, and language. Thai cooking places emphasis on lightly prepared dishes with strong aromatic components and a spicy edge.
